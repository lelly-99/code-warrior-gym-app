BarBell

Instructions

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing forward. Keep your upper arms stationary and exhale as you curl the dumbbells towards your shoulders, contracting your biceps. Pause briefly at the top of the movement, squeezing your biceps. Inhale as you slowly lower the dumbbells back to the starting position in a controlled manner. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

Target Muscle

Target Muscle Group: Biceps (front of the upper arm). Pairing Equipment: Dumbbell bicep curls can be paired with exercises like hammer curls or preacher curls to target the biceps from different angles.
